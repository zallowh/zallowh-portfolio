Grocery Delivery App – MVP launch, UX Case Study

Designed for users who lack the time or ability to shop in person, this mobile app brings trusted grocery brands directly to their doorstep with a clean, store-like experience.

Product Design Focus

I focused on solving real user pain points by enabling:

  • Frictionless onboarding and multi-option login (email, Google, Apple, Facebook)
  • Category-based browsing, smart search, and a persistent shopping cart
  • Safe, familiar checkout using in-store pricing and secure payments
  • Quick service access (airtime, data, loyalty tracking) and responsive customer support

User Experience Strategy

The UX prioritizes clarity, speed, and user control. The home screen adapts to both new and returning users, while full account and order tracking provides transparency. Every screen is designed to reduce cognitive load and build trust.
